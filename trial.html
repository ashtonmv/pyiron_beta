--- title: Try pyiron yourself on a remote MyBinder instance description: ---

  1. Start the MyBinder instance here. The bootup can take 2-5 minutes as it installs the latest (beta) version of pyiron from the repository.

  2. Open the first_steps notebook and follow the tutorial at your own pace.

  3. Experiment with your own modifications and new code! You can even create a new notebook from scratch on the MyBinder instance - just be aware that MyBinder is meant for testing, so once you close the instance your changes will be lost.
